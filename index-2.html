About US

Our House - an old factory building from the turn of the century - was restored and developed in
1995. The typical Berlin-Kreuzberg charme of a brick building has been retained with light, spacious
rooms and a courtyard garden. On each floor you can find large bathrooms.

our facilities

We offer a great list of facilties for your own comfort so you can have a great time at our hostel.

1
Free Outdoor Swimming Pool

Our outdoor swimming pool with waterfall is perfect to laze by on those 35 degree days when lining up at a museum.
2
Free Use Of Fitness Equipment

We have cardio fitness equipment free to all guests - to help you with your pizza andpasta guilt.
3
Free Linen

Free linen for all of our guests. In an effort to minimise hygiene risks, sleeping bags are not allowed to be used.
4
Opera Bar & Restaurant

Party in our very own basement bar! You'll also be able to grab traditional Italian food without breaking your budget.

5
Opera Terrace Bar

The panoramic terrace is just perfect for that sundowner at the end of the day. So feel free to enjoy it 100%!
6
Laundry Facilities

We have great washing machines and dryers for yours (and your fellow travellers') benefit. Everything just for you!
